About

The Sit & Spin Laundry Cafe was founded in 2020, and is located in Seattle, Washington. We provide an array of laundry services, a delicious cafe menu, and live entertainment on select nights throughout the week.

Laundry

Come by anytime and use our clean machines! You can use quarters or sign up for a card that you can load money on.

We also offer wash and fold services, drop off anytime and pick up within 24 hours! We'll let you know when it's ready, via phone call or text message!

Cafe

Meet our friendly staff and partake in sweet or savory snacks. We employ some of the best baristas in the city!

Live entertainment is usually scheduled for Tuesday, Thursday, Friday, and Saturday nights. On rare occasions, there will be a small cover charge. Are you interested in performing? Contact us!

Hours

We are open 24/7 to serve the community. Occasionally we will close for staff training and enrichment.

Staff

Our staff members are friendly, helpful, and flexible. In addition to laundry attendants, there is always one or more at the cafe to provide you with drinks and snacks, to fuel your morning/afternoon/evening/late night! We also employ security personnel, especially at night, and our parking lot is well lit. The owners deal with booking the entertainment. We're here for you and we appreciate your business.
